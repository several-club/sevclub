Cucina Povera

Cucina Povera is a new restaurant in the heart of Stockholm. We created various printed and digital materials for the launch of this restaurant. The first thing we did was to create a brand identity that reflected the concept and philosophy of this place which is based on simple yet delicious food.

We wanted to create a rustic and warm design that was both inviting and effortless. The second step was designing their menu, which is elegant and allows users to navigate easily through all the information they need.

Sure, Cervera is a well-known brand with an established identity. Still, we wanted the giftshop to feel a bit more exclusive than their regular visual expression, so we put on our designer caps and created a modified (and more elegant) style guide for Cervera Giftshop.
