Snacka om Sjukt!

Snacka om Sjukt! provides pedagogic, interactive lectures for school students, breaking taboos around illness through open conversations and storytelling. Their mission is to inspire understanding and empower young people to navigate health challenges. We designed and developed a dynamic, user-friendly website tailored to multiple target groups, including students, educators, decision-makers, and parents. The site focuses on storytelling to highlight their problem-and-solution-driven message. We also designed engaging social media content to broaden their reach. The result is a cohesive and versatile digital presence that fosters meaningful connections and drives interaction.
