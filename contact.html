Several Services

Design as a Service

At Several, we offer you the opportunity to access a team of designers and communication specialists through our "Design as a Service" solution. Tailor your package with the flexibility of selecting a time bank that suits your needs, all at a fixed rate per hour. Save unused hours for the upcoming month for full flexibility. As an adaptable extension of your marketing department, we excel at handling creative processes and production with technical expertise, strategic insight, and visual finesse.

Branding Per Project

You also have the option to choose Branding per Project, a more project-based solution that offers a comprehensive brand upgrade, including visual identity, logo design, and brand guidelines. Regardless of the solution you select, all of these services are delivered within a clear and fixed cost structure.

Minimum Viable Brand

Our customized packages provide basic brand development for startups and extensive brand transformation for established companies, with a focus on strengthening and rejuvenating brands. This package includes essential brand elements such as a logo, a simple website, and a few social media templates. It is perfect for newly launched businesses or those seeking a straightforward and effective brand foundation.

+
-

Scale Up Brand Package

Taking a step further from the Minimal Valuable Brand, the Scale Up Brand Package includes document templates, social media templates, basic brand guidelines, and a concise brand platform for clear positioning. It is ideal for companies ready to expand with a strong brand identity.

+
-

Full Identity Rebrand

This is our most comprehensive package, perfect for established companies in need of a complete brand overhaul. It includes everything in the Scale Up Brand Package,  along with a comprehensive brand platform, cohesive visual identity, and detailed brand guidelines, enriched with an element of sonic branding for auditory recognition.
